About Me

My passion for technology started at a young age and has grown into a strong interest in web development, where I enjoy designing and building websites that are both functional and visually engaging. I’m also fascinated by how digital systems work and love exploring new tools and technologies.

Outside of coding, I’m a huge fan of video games—not just for entertainment, but also as a way to learn about user interface design and interactivity. I also enjoy traveling, especially to historically significant or culturally rich destinations, where I can connect with stories and architecture from the past. Through this portfolio, I'm documenting my learning journey and showcasing the skills and projects I’m proud of as I continue to grow in the IT field.
